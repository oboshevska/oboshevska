Vidi Vaka Website Redesign

Company / Brand Overview

Vidi Vaka is a medium that sole purpose is to inform the digital generation which consumes news from online sources. Vidi Vaka is a self producing, writing and publishing medium that shares their news stories mainly on their social media profiles.
Their way of presenting the news is not connected with day-to-day news broadcasts or is politically involved. Their main focus are people and news worth sharring.

Brief - Goals and Objectives

Storytelling. Vidi Vaka's soecialty is to find and share a story to their public. For that purpose, the website needs to be user friendly for a wide audience.



The project needs to achieve:

  • Clean design
  • A good functional website
  • Home page and some sub pages
  • Easy to manage
  • User Friendly
  • Completely new User Experience

Website Redesign

There is an existing website which the design is outdated. The new design must have a dynamic feel. To show off the work and stories worth sharing in high quality content.
The website would be the main medium to the new and refular readers.
In some ways, the new design should be viewed as a portfolio on Vidi Vaka's stories and news. And finally, to attract new clients and build and improve its reputation.

Competitors Analysis

Based on the competitive analisys, the main characteristics that appear to all of them are:

  • Links to social media on the header section
  • Tags on the titles
  • Option for language selection
  • Original Content
  • Splitscreen stories

User Personas

The target audience is broad and targets many groups such as: young adults,regular readers,business ownersand clients.So, based on the competitors features, user reviews and stories, we created a fictional characters on our close to ideal users on the medium website.

Martina is our primary user persona and she is a project manager. Since her life is busy and energetic, she would like to read the news on the palm of her hand.

Filip is also our primary user persona and he is a potential client and owner of a business that can hire the team about his marketing or stories about his budiness.

Hristo is our secondary user persona and he is a reporter. He is looking for a fast way to write and report on the stories.

Conclusion & Takeaways

Working in team and having a great communication was a key feature for this project sucess.
Since redesigning a website from reference from an already excisting one was something new to me, i expected and got really good constructive feedback on the design from the rest of the team.

As a learning experience i can say i improved my communication skills, i learnt not to overdesign or use many prototyping features ( less is more).
Another learning experience was improving my technical skills in Figma and the design overall.

However, in future projects, i would pay more attention to details, conduct user interviews, gather more data and spend more time on the research itself.
