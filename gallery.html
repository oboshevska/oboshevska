Gallery '94 Website Design

Company / Brand Overview

A platform for selling artwork and art equipment on domestic and foreign markets.
Gallery '94 is a place to sell artwork at its best price.

My Role

User Research
UI Design
Prototyping &

Brief - Goals and Objectives

Since there are no good functional platforms on the domestic markets for promoting Macedonian Artwork on a global level, Gallery '94's goal is to promote Macedonian Artwork by selling it online.
The company's mission is to sale and promote Macedonian Artists and nurture the Macedonian Art Culture.


The project needs to achieve:

  • Minimalistic e-commerce website
  • A good functional e-commerce website
  • Had to be on two languages : MK and ENG
  • Easy to manage
  • User Friendly
  • Easy to manage for the Admin and content writes

Website Redesign

There is an already non functional solution for the website set up in Squarespace, but since that online platform is not likely to be used in the domestic market, the website needs to be designed from scratch in a new program.

Target Market or Audience

All profiles of people, ages 21+.
Gallery owners, Gallery Artists, Artists, Institutions. 🎨

Competitors from Macedonia

The competitors are physical galleries for selling art based on the local market - Macedonia.
The advante is global outreach, minimalistic with a touch of modern and nice preview of the art.
Not so many pages or sections.

The process - User Interview Questions

Questions:

  1. How often do you shop online?
  2. Do you shop online from their web stores or use social media?
  3. What are your frustrations when buying online?
  4. How would you rate e-shops in macedonia and what will you improve?
  5. Have you ever bought some kind of art or art equipment online?
  6. Would you be interested in information about the artist?
  7. Which other categories would you like to see on the website, which are not there right now?

User Interview Answers

By sending the user interview question to some pc/mobile shoppers, we got multiple responces that would help our design.

All of our users shop online, but 90% of the users shop directly on the shop's offical website.

Some of the frustrations that the users had were that there are no images displaying the products or product care description.

Competitors Analysis

PROS:

  1. Clean and simple design
  2. User friendly platform
  3. Artist of the week featured
  4. Artist promotion
  5. Wide variety of products

CONS:

  1. Crowded home page
  2. Too much text on the artist’s page
  3. Lack of interaction
  4. Not enough info about the products or artists
  5. Have you ever bought some kind of art or art equipment online?
  6. Would you be interested in information about the artist?
  7. Mixed languages on the site

User Personas

Based on reading user interviews, researching the competitors features and products, we created a fictional character on our close to ideal user of our e-commerce website.

Vesna is our primary user persona and she is an artist. Since we are designing a webshop, one of the personas is the artist whose art is for selling and buying art equipment.

Stefan is our secondary user persona and he is the buyer of art.

Conclusion & Takeaways

Working in team and having a great communication was a key feature for this project sucess.
Since designing a website from scratch was something new to me, i expected and got really good constructive feedback on the design from the rest of the team.

As a learning experience i can say i improved my communication skills, i learnt not to overdesign or use many prototyping features ( less is more).
Another learning experience was improving my technical skills in Figma and the design overall.

However, in future projects, i would pay more attention to details, conduct user interviews, gather more data and spend more time on the research itself.
